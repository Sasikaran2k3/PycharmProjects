Is Crypto-Sceptic Apple Circulating Bitcoin Whitepaper in MacBook Models Running MacOS Mojave and Above? 
It is no secret that Apple has tried to remain rather neutral about its opinions on cryptocurrencies, having kept its brand identity detached from any association with crypto so far. A recent finding by American technologist Andy Baio has, however, discovered a thread of relation between Apple and the world's first and most expensive cryptocurrency — Bitcoin. The Apple products linked with BTC are MacBook models that are running the 2018 Mojave operating system and above.
