Price for the Tecno Camon 20 Premier 5G Has Been Rumoured for India Launch on July 7 
The Tecno Camon 20 Premier 5G will debut on July 7 in India. The phone debuted earlier this year in a few international regions alongside the Tecno Camon 20 and Tecno Camon 20 Pro 5G variants of the Tecno Camon 20 series. The 8GB + 128GB Tecno Camon 20 Pro variant is listed at Rs. 19,999, while the 8GB + 256GB Camon 20 variant costs Rs. 14,999. A tipster has offered a pricing prediction for the Camon 20 Premier before it is introduced in India.
