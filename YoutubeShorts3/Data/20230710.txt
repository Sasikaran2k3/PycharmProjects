Comparison of the price and features of the OnePlus Nord CE 3 with the Samsung Galaxy M34 in India 
On July 7, the Samsung Galaxy M34 was introduced in India with a starting price of Rs. 20,000. The smartphone, which replaces the Galaxy M33, is powered by One UI 5 on Android 13. It is driven by an Exynos 1280 SoC and has up to 128GB of inbuilt storage in addition to 8GB of RAM. OnePlus had its Summer Launch event, where the Chinese company unveiled the OnePlus Nord CE 3, two days prior to the introduction of Samsung's smartphone. This OnePlus device features a triple camera configuration and a 5,000mAh battery with support for 80W SUPERVOOC charging.
