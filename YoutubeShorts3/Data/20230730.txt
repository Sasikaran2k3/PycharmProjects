Prices and features of the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 5 and Galaxy S23 Ultra in India 
At the company's Galaxy Unpacked event in July, the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 5 was unveiled. The smartphone, which replaces the Galaxy Z Flip 4 from last year, is powered by a specialised Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 SoC. A 3,700mAh battery powers it. This year's introduction of the Galaxy S23 series was unveiled at a second Galaxy Unpacked event held by the South Korean tech titan in February. There were three smartphones in the lineup: the Galaxy S23, Galaxy S23+, and Galaxy S23 Ultra. The top-of-the-line Galaxy S23 Ultra also has a modified Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 SoC from Qualcomm.
