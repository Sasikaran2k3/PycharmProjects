To allow the ruling against Apple's App Store payment policies to take effect, Epic Games petitions the US Supreme Court. 
On Thursday, Epic Games petitioned the US Supreme Court to permit the implementation of a lower court decision against Apple, which might compel the iPhone manufacturer to alter its App Store payment policies.
