The release date for Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 on Disney+ Hotstar is set for August 2. 
On Wednesday, August 2, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 will be available on Disney+ and Disney+ Hotstar. The Marvels, which will premiere on November 10 worldwide, will follow the most recent installment of the Marvel Cinematic Universe 89 days after its May 5 theatrical debut in India, the US, and other countries. The 90-day window Disney established for Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania, the studio's most recent film, is one day less than that. On Disney+ Hotstar in India, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 will be viewable in English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam.
