Microsoft requests a two-month adjournment of its Activision deal appeal with the London court. 
Microsoft requested on Monday that its appeal against the British government's decision to prohibit its $69 billion (about Rs. 5,66,100 crore) acquisition of Activision Blizzard be placed on hold in order to give the parties more time to work out their differences.
