Realme 11 Pro 5G vs Motorola Edge 40: Price in India, Specifications Compared 
Realme 11 Pro 5G was launched in India on June 8 this year, along with the debut of Realme 11 Pro+ 5G. The smartphone has a 6.7-inch full-HD+ (1,080 x 2,412 pixels) curved display. It is powered by a 6nm MediaTek Dimensity 7050 SoC. With up to 12GB of RAM and up to 512GB of inbuilt storage, the smartphone is available in three colour variants. Meanwhile, Motorola Edge 40 was launched in India in May 2023. It is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 8020 SoC, with single RAM and storage configuration. This smartphone is also available in three colour options.
